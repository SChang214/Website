Exoskeleton Hand | Biomechatronics Design Team

Mechanial, SolidWorks, 3D Printing | 2025

Developed an exoskeleton hand in the mechanical sub team of Bio-mechatronics.

Designed a low-cost exoskeleton hand to help people with low hand strength (ie. muscle dystropy) carry things.

Contributions

- Collaboratively and iteratively designed a low-cost mechanic exoskeleton hand which cost $10 to aid people with low hand strength using SolidWorks

- Designed and modeled 1 degree of freedom joints in SolidWorks to optimize parts spacing.

- Rapidly prototyped a pin locking mechanism in SolidWorks to provide external structural support for the hand

Current status

- The current prototype is mechanical based, in future iterations we are looking into incorporating servo motors into the design to make it more automated
