Nothern Student-Led Arctic Research Project

Research | 2023-2025

Participated in research concerning the health of the Western Hudson Bay polar bear population.

Worked in a team supervised by Dr. Waterman, Dr. Roth and Dr. McKinnon from the University of Manitoba to analyse polar bear health and compare 3 methods to evaluate polar bear health.

This analysis used the data of 88 bears.

This is a long term project that comprises of more than 27 years of data which searches to unobtrusively monitor polar health in the Western Hudson Bay polar bear population.
